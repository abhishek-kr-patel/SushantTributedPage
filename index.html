Tribute to Sushant Singh

Introduction

Sushant Singh Rajput was an Indian actor known for his work in Hindi cinema. He starred in a number of commercially successful Hindi films such as M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story, Kedarnath and Chhichhore. He was born on 21 Jan,1986, in Patna,Bihar was one of the most brilliant minds in human history.
